About TPLF

Meet the Team behind thisplacelooksfamiliar.com

The main purpose of our website is to provide visitors with information on buildings set in Ireland that have been featured in movies. We will provide comprehensive information including historical and cultural background, detailed visit plans, transportation, ticket information, opening hours, etc. We hope that visitors will discover unique places to visit in Ireland through this website and gain a practical travel plan.

This website is being developed by 5 students currently undertaking a M.Sc in Interactive Digital Media at Trinity College Dublin. The module this assignment falls under is CS7026 Authoring for Digital Media, currently taught by Nina Breshihan, Assistant Professor in the School of Computer Science & Statistics.
